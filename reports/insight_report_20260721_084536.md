## Question: What prevents users from exploring new categories?

### Executive Summary: Barriers to Exploring New Categories on Blinkit
Based on the aggregated data from Blinkit user reviews, the key factors preventing users from exploring new categories are related to accessibility and technical issues. 

* The sentiment around exploring new categories is entirely negative, indicating significant frustration among users.
* The top pain points mentioned are:
  + Area not available, highlighting geographical limitations.
  + Payment and OTP issues, suggesting technical hurdles.
* Users are specifically requesting expansion into new areas (e.g., Virudhunagar) and expressing broader difficulties with logistics in India, implying that geographical restrictions and operational challenges are primary barriers to exploration.
